Anticipate the type, trajectory and the remaining time of the instrument in the next 1/3/5 min, using only laparoscopic video as input and design an interpretable module to encode the video features.

Study the newly proposed pruning strategies for the neural networks. We proposed a new regularization term to make network sparse during the training process instead of pruning after the training process. Experiment shows that the proposed regularization term is comparable with the other pruning methods when only 10 % of weights are kept.It also need less time to find the sub-network that performs as good as the full network.

Reimplement the method proposed in paper «Grounding of Textual Phrases in Images by Reconstruction». Transfer the graph-based method proposed in «Language-Conditioned Graph Networks for Relational Reasoning» into the Flickr30k dataset. The project is still in progress and public at https://github.com/Flaick/GroundR
